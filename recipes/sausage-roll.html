Sausage French Toast Roll-Ups

Description

Sausage French Toast Roll-Ups are a delicious breakfast option that combines the best of both savory and sweet flavors.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or brioche)
  • 8 sausage links (pork, turkey, or vegetarian)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Butter for frying
  • Maple syrup or powdered sugar for serving

Steps

  1. Cook: Start by cooking the sausage links in a skillet over medium heat until fully cooked. If you prefer a healthier option, try using turkey or vegetarian sausage. Once cooked, set the sausages aside and allow them to cool slightly.
  2. Prep: In a shallow bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla extract. This mixture will coat the bread and give the roll-ups their signature French toast flavor.
  3. Roll: Trim the crusts from the bread slices to make rolling easier. Using a rolling pin, flatten each slice of bread. Place a cooked sausage link on one end of each slice and roll tightly. Make sure to seal the roll-ups by gently pressing the seam side down.
  4. Dip and Cook: Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add a small amount of butter. Dip each roll-up into the French toast batter, making sure it is fully coated. Place the roll-ups seam-side down in the skillet and cook, turning occasionally, until all sides are golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve: Once cooked, serve the Sausage French Toast Roll-Ups with a drizzle of maple syrup or a dusting of powdered sugar. You can also add fresh fruit or whipped cream for extra indulgence.
